ABOUT ME

I was born in Salt Lake City, Utah, and have been blessed to grow up surrounded by the strong values and close-knit community that have shaped who I am today. My faith in God, love for people, and desire to grow have guided me throughout my life. I graduated from Woods Cross High School in 2020 with a 3.8 GPA. I enjoyed the educational experience, made amazing friends, and spent much of my time performing—something I’ve loved doing throughout my entire life.

Following high school, I attended BYU–Idaho, where I earned my associate degree and began studying general business. During that time, I gained valuable experience with tools like Microsoft Excel and developed a greater understanding of the business world. I am now continuing my education at BYU–Provo, where I’m pursuing a business degree with the goal of being accepted into the marketing program. I'm drawn to marketing because it allows me to blend creativity with strategy while connecting with others in meaningful ways.

A defining part of my life was serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I loved sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ and building genuine relationships with people from all walks of life. Later, I had the unique opportunity to serve a performing mission in Nauvoo, Illinois. There, I portrayed early members of the Church and shared their stories through music, dance, and storytelling—an experience that deepened both my testimony and my love for performing.

At my core, I’m someone who values faith, family, friendship, education, and creative expression. I strive to approach life with positivity, diligence, and a sincere desire to uplift those around me. Whether in a classroom, on a stage, or working with a team, I’m passionate about using my talents and experiences to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.
