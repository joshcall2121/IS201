MY MUSICAL THEATER JOURNEY

I began performing at the age of five, and from that moment on, I was hooked. My whole family is incredibly musical, and we’ve always enjoyed performing together, which has made music and theater an integral part of my life. Through musical theater, I’ve not only cultivated my artistic talents but also developed lasting friendships and honed essential social skills that have shaped who I am today.

Throughout high school, I actively pursued my passion for performing, excelling in various school plays and musicals. In my junior year, I was honored to be nominated for Best Supporting Actor, a recognition that filled me with pride and motivated me to continue striving for excellence. My passion for performing also led me to serve as a Nauvoo Performing Missionary during the summer of 2024, where I had the incredible opportunity to portray historical church figures through song and dance, sharing their stories with others.

Performing has always been more than just an activity for me—it’s a way for me to use the talents God has blessed me with and inspire others through my art. I am committed to continuing my journey in musical theater, and I look forward to all the opportunities and experiences it will bring as I grow both as an artist and as a person.

Josh Call
